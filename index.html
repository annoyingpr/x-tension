Ergonomics Tips!


1. Keep moving!

Regularly vary your body positioning.

2. Posture

Maintain good head and neck posture throughout the day.

3. Eyes on the prize!

Combat eye strain with the 20-20-20 rule.

4. No slouching allowed!

Keep an eye on your back posture, and adjust your chair accordingly.

5. Arms

Make sure that the weight of your arms is supported at all times.

6. No squinting!

Ensure your head and neck aren't "craned" forward to your screen.

7. Chair placement

Move your chair as close to your work as possible to avoid leaning and reaching.

8. Looking forward

Your monitor should be placed directly in front of you, with the top no higher than eye level.

9. Keyboard whereabouts

Your keyboard should be directly in front of your monitor.

10. Feet!

Your feet should not be dangling when you are seated. Use a footrest if neccessary.

11. Glare be gone

Control screen glare by purchasing anti-glare glasses or using light-filters on your device.

12. Work hard, play hard

Setup different areas in your home for work hours and recreation, and use them appropriately!
